What is a Chiropractor?
A chiropractor is focused on the body’s ability to self-heal by helping to manage pain through the use of adjustments to maintain good alignment by realigning the joints in the body. Chiropractic adjustments improve the functioning of other systems throughout the body. Chiropractors can help with a multitude of conditions and are used as an alternative to, or to complement, conventional medicine by keeping people off drugs and away from surgery unless absolutely necessary and as a last resort.
