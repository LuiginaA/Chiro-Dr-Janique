About

Meet Dr Janique Belli

I studied Chiropractic at the University of Johannesburg, South Africa, which is also accredited under the European Council of Chiropractic. I qualified with a Masters Degree. Nearing the end of my studies I set out to find a mentor that would guide me both professionally and personally. I am registered with the Allied Health Professions Council of South Africa (AHPCSA) and a member of the Chiropractic Association of South Africa (CASA) and Paediatric Chiropractic South Africa (PCSA). I was referred to a well known Chiropractor in the northern suburbs who showed me different techniques and enhanced my chiropractic profession extensively.

Mission Statement

With passion and compassion, ”My Mission“ is in helping others through a positive and mental attitude by providing chiropractic and wellness care to all my patients.
